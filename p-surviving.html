Surviving
you, Always

Surviving you, always is a collaboration between Collettivo Treppenwitz and LAC Lugano Arte e Cultura. The director, Simon Waldvogel, envisioned a hybrid performance between a live set, a visual installation and poetry tackling loss and memory. We were tasked to create a graphic concept that included flyers, social media content, photographs and videos.

Inspired by the script, we chose to focus on three main elements: the skin as a place of rawness and memory, photographic slides, representing renegotiation with the past, and the discoball as the celebration of loss. These are reflected in the graphics, photographs and videos that we made throught the collaboration.
