I'm Luna, an Italo-Japanese designer based in Milan. I specialise in brand identity and audiovisual design, encompassing fields such as motion design, 3D animation, video production, and photography.

I am always exploring new tools and visual languages to bring my projects to life. Beyond design, I draw inspiration from the things I love, including films, music, books, and illustration, which continuously shape my creative vision.

Feel free to contact me on any social or email, for any type of inquiry :)
